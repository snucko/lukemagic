**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Water Drop"**

**Materials:**

1. A clear glass or cup (at least 6-8 oz capacity)
2. A small bowl or saucer
3. A dropper or a small straw
4. Water
5. A handkerchief or a small piece of cloth
6. A small, flat object (e.g., a coin, a button, or a small piece of paper)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Fill the glass or cup with about 2-3 oz of water.
2. Place the small bowl or saucer on a flat surface nearby.
3. Fill the dropper or straw with a small amount of water.
4. Place the handkerchief or cloth on the table, folded in half.

1. Show the audience the glass or cup filled with water.
