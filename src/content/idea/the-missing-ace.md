**Trick Name: "The Missing Ace"**

**Materials:**

- A standard deck of 52 playing cards
- A small piece of paper or a sticky note
- A pen or pencil

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. Shuffle the deck of cards thoroughly to mix up the order.
2. Ask a spectator to choose a card from the deck, and have them sign their name across the face of the card to ensure it's clearly identifiable as their selection.
3. Take the signed card from the spectator and place it on top of the deck.
4. Place the deck on a flat surface in front of you.
5. Take the small piece of paper or sticky note and write the following message on it: "The Ace of Hearts is hidden in plain sight."
6. Fold the paper or sticky note in half and place it on top of the deck, on top of the signed card.
7. Ask the spectator to look through the deck and find their signed card.
