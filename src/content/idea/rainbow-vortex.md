**Trick Name: Rainbow Vortex**

**Materials:**

1. A clear, sealed envelope with a prediction written on a piece of paper inside (e.g., "The chosen color is Blue").
2. A set of 6 colored cards or stickers (Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, and Purple).
3. A small, transparent plastic container or a mini globe (about 2-3 inches in diameter).
4. A piece of string or a small elastic band.
5. A small, decorative box or a small gift box.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Write the prediction on a piece of paper and seal it inside the clear envelope.
2. Place the colored cards or stickers in a random order on a table or surface.
3. Attach the string or elastic band to the small container or globe, so it can be easily lifted and dropped.

1. Show the sealed envelope to the audience and explain that it contains a prediction about the chosen color.
