**Trick Name: "Timeless Fate"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, blank note card or a piece of paper
2. A pen or pencil
3. A small, sealed envelope (preferably with a unique address or a secret code)
4. A deck of ordinary playing cards (without jokers)
5. A small, decorative box or a small container with a lid (e.g., a small glass jar or a wooden box)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Before the performance, write a random number (1-10) on the blank note card. Fold the card and seal it in the envelope. Make sure the address or secret code on the envelope is unique and not easily guessable.
2. Shuffle the deck of playing cards and place it on a table or surface.
3. Place the small, decorative box or container on the table, with the lid slightly ajar.
