**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Floating Orb"**

**Props:**

1. A clear glass or plastic orb (about 6-8 inches in diameter)
2. A small LED light source (e.g., a tiny LED strip or a small LED light)
3. A hidden compartment or a small box with a magnetic lid
4. A thin, flexible wire (e.g., a coat hanger wire)
5. A small piece of black cloth or a blindfold
6. A table or a pedestal to display the orb

**Setup:**

1. Place the hidden compartment or small box with a magnetic lid on the table or pedestal, covered with the black cloth or blindfold.
2. Place the LED light source inside the hidden compartment.
3. Attach the thin, flexible wire to the underside of the table or pedestal, making sure it's not visible from the audience's perspective.
4. Place the orb on the table or pedestal, about 6-8 inches away from the hidden compartment.
