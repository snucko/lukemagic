**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Memory Deck"**

**Materials:**

* A standard deck of 52 playing cards
* A small piece of paper and a pen
* A small envelope or a sealed envelope with no windows

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Before the performance, secretly write down the name of a card (e.g., the 5 of Hearts) on the piece of paper and seal it in the envelope. Make sure the envelope is not visible to the audience.
2. **Shuffle the Deck:** Shuffle the deck of cards to mix them up. This will ensure that the cards are in a random order.
3. **Ask the Audience to Choose a Card:** Ask a spectator to choose a card from the deck at random. Make sure they sign their name across the face of the card to ensure it's clearly identifiable as their selection.
4. **Return the Card to the Deck:** Have the spectator return their chosen card to the deck.
5. **Riffle Shuffle:** Perform a riffle shuffle to mix the cards again. This will help to conceal the location of the chosen card.
