**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Butterfly"**

**Props:**

1. A beautiful, artificially created butterfly (made of lightweight, flexible materials such as foam, fabric, or plastic) with a wire or thread attached to its body.
2. A small, decorative box with a lid (about 6-8 inches in length, 4-6 inches in width, and 2-3 inches in height).
3. A small, hidden compartment or secret drawer in the box (can be created using a DIY kit or purchased separately).
4. A length of thin, colored ribbon or string.
5. A small, decorative flower or leaf (optional).

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Place the butterfly in the hidden compartment of the box.
2. Tie the ribbon or string around the lid of the box, securing it with a small knot.
3. Place the flower or leaf on top of the box, if using.

**Performance:**

1. Hold the box in one hand, with the ribbon or string tied around the lid.
2. Show the audience the butterfly on top of the box, and then slowly lift the lid to reveal the empty box.
3. Close the lid and shake the box
