**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Loop"**

**Materials:**

* A sturdy, colorful rope or string (at least 6 feet long)
* A small, smooth object to use as a "key" (e.g., a small metal ring, a bead, or a small stone)
* A table or other stable surface

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Introduction**: Hold the rope in one hand, with the ends hanging loose. Explain to your audience that you will perform a simple magic trick with the rope.
2. **The Loop**: Create a loop in the rope by folding it back onto itself, with the ends hanging loose. Hold the loop in one hand, with the ends dangling.
3. **The Key**: Secretly place the small object (key) inside the loop, making sure it's not visible to the audience.
4. **The Vanish**: Hold the loop in one hand, with the ends hanging loose. Use your other hand to pull one end of the rope through the loop, creating a new loop. As you do this, secretly drop the key from the original loop to the table or surface below.
5. **The Reveal**: Hold up the new loop, with the ends still hanging
