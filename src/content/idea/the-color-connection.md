**Trick Name: "The Color Connection"**

**Materials:**

1. A sealed envelope with a white piece of paper inside.
2. A set of 6 colored cards (red, blue, green, yellow, purple, and orange).
3. A small piece of white paper and a pen.
4. A transparent plastic bag.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Before the performance, write a single color on the white piece of paper inside the sealed envelope. This will be your prediction.
2. Place the colored cards in the transparent plastic bag, making sure they are not visible.
3. Give the sealed envelope to a spectator to hold onto.

**Performance:**

1. Ask the spectator to choose a color from the transparent plastic bag without looking inside.
2. The spectator should then write the chosen color on the small piece of white paper.
3. Take the small piece of paper from the spectator and place it on top of the sealed envelope.
4. Now, ask the spectator to open the sealed envelope and reveal the prediction inside.
5. The prediction should match the color written on the small piece of paper.

**The Secret:**

The key to this trick is to use a technique called "forcing."
