**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Flower"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, artificial flower (preferably with a removable center)
2. A small, hidden compartment in a wooden or plastic box (you can purchase a pre-made box or create one yourself)
3. A small piece of clear plastic or a glass vial with a stopper
4. A handkerchief or small piece of cloth
5. A small amount of confetti or glitter (optional)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. Begin by placing the artificial flower in the hidden compartment of the box. Make sure the compartment is secure and the flower is fully enclosed.
2. Show the audience the empty box and the flower, explaining that you will make the flower disappear.
3. Place the handkerchief or cloth over the box and pretend to place the flower inside.
4. Lift the handkerchief or cloth, and to the audience's surprise, the flower has vanished.
5. Now, here's the twist: secretly place the clear plastic or glass vial with the stopper into the hidden compartment. This will create a mini "time capsule" effect.
6. Explain to the audience that you will now reveal the location of the flower. Place
