**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Floating Book"**

**Props:**

1. A small, decorative wooden box (approx. 6 inches long, 4 inches wide, and 2 inches high)
2. A small, leather-bound book (approx. 4 inches long, 2 inches wide, and 1 inch thick)
3. A small, hidden compartment in the box (can be created using a small piece of wood or a hidden drawer)
4. A thin, flexible wire (e.g., a paperclip or a thin metal wire)
5. A small, magnetic button or a small piece of metal (hidden in the book)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Place the small, leather-bound book inside the wooden box.
2. Attach the thin, flexible wire to the bottom of the box, making sure it's not visible from the outside.
3. Hide the small, magnetic button or piece of metal inside the book.

**Performance:**

1. Open the box, revealing the book inside.
2. Hold the box in one hand, with the book facing upwards.
3. Use your other hand to lift the book out of the box, making it seem as if it's floating
