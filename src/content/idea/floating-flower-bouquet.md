**Trick Name: "Floating Flower Bouquet"**

**Materials:**

1. A beautiful, artificial flower bouquet with a removable base (at least 2-3 flowers)
2. A small, hidden compartment box (you can make your own or purchase one)
3. A velvet cloth or a black tablecloth
4. A small, hidden thread or fishing line (thin and almost invisible)
5. A small, hidden button or magnet (to secure the thread)

**Setup:**

1. Place the hidden compartment box under the velvet cloth or tablecloth.
2. Attach the hidden thread to the base of the flower bouquet.
3. Secure the thread to the hidden button or magnet inside the compartment box.
4. Place the flower bouquet on top of the velvet cloth or tablecloth, making sure it's centered.

**Performance:**

1. Begin by introducing the flower bouquet and explaining that you'll be making it float in mid-air.
2. Hold the bouquet in one hand and slowly lift it up, keeping your hand steady.
3. As you lift the bouquet, use your other hand to secretly pull the thread, making the bouquet appear to be floating.
