**Trick Name: "Floating Petals"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, decorative flower pot (about 3-4 inches in height)
2. A set of small, dried flower petals (any color)
3. A small, hidden compartment (e.g., a small wooden or plastic box with a lid)
4. A small piece of cloth or a handkerchief
5. A small, hidden thread or string

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Place the small flower pot on a table or surface in front of you.
2. Fill the hidden compartment with a few dried flower petals.
3. Tie the small thread or string to the lid of the hidden compartment, making sure it's secure and won't come loose during the performance.
4. Place the small piece of cloth or handkerchief on the table, next to the flower pot.
