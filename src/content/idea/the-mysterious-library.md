**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Library"**

**Props:**

1. A small, ornate wooden box with a hinged lid (approx. 6 inches long, 4 inches wide, and 2 inches deep)
2. A set of 5-6 small, identical-looking books with different colored covers (approx. 4 inches long, 2 inches wide, and 1 inch deep)
3. A small, silver key (approx. 2 inches long)
4. A small, velvet-covered table or pedestal (approx. 12 inches long, 6 inches wide, and 2 inches high)
5. A small, hidden compartment in the table or pedestal (accessible from underneath)

**Setup:**

1. Place the small, ornate wooden box on the velvet-covered table or pedestal.
2. Arrange the set of small, identical-looking books on a nearby shelf or on the table, but not in the box.
3. Place the small, silver key on the table, but not in the box.
4. Make sure the hidden compartment in the table or pedestal is accessible from underneath.
