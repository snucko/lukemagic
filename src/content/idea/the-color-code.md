**Trick Name: "The Color Code"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, blank box or envelope (approx. 5 inches long, 3 inches wide, and 1 inch deep)
2. 6 small, identical cards or paper slips with different colors written on them (red, blue, green, yellow, orange, and purple)
3. A piece of paper or a small notebook for the prediction
4. A pen or pencil

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Before the performance, write a prediction on the piece of paper or in the notebook. This can be a simple statement, such as: "The randomly selected color will be blue."
2. Fold the paper or notebook to conceal the prediction and place it in the small box or envelope.

**Performance:**

1. Shuffle the 6 colored cards and ask a spectator to select one at random.
2. Show the spectator the remaining 5 cards and ask them to confirm that they are all different colors.
3. Take the selected card from the spectator and place it on top of the deck.
