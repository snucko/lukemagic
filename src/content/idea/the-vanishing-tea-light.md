**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Tea Light"**

**Materials:**

- 1 tea light or small candle
- 1 small plate or saucer
- 1 large plate or tray
- 1 small piece of cloth or paper towel
- 1 rubber band
- A small amount of tape

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Place the small plate or saucer on the large plate or tray. This will be the base of the trick.

2. **The Setup:** Place the tea lightTmporarily on the small plate or saucer. Cover the tea light with the small piece of cloth or paper towel. Secure the cloth with a small piece of tape.

3. **The Performance:** Place the covered tea light on the large plate or tray. Show the audience the tea light and explain that you will make it disappear.

4. **The Disappearance:** Place the rubber band around the large plate or tray, covering the tea light. Slowly lift the rubber band, and as you do, use your other hand to secretly drop the tea light onto the floor or a nearby surface, out of sight.
