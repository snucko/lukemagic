**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Coin in the Pocket"**

**Materials:**

- A quarter or any other coin that fits comfortably in your pocket
- A small piece of cloth or a handkerchief (optional)
- A table or a flat surface

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Place the coin in your right pocket (if you're right-handed). Make sure it's hidden from view.
2. **Misdirection:** Hold your right hand out, palm up, and show the audience that your hand is empty. This is your misdirection.
3. **Coin Placement:** With your left hand, discreetly place the coin on the back of your right hand, near the wrist. This is a basic sleight of hand move called a "palm."
4. **Coin Transfer:** As you show the audience your empty right hand, use your left hand to secretly transfer the coin to your left pocket. This is another sleight of hand move called a "false transfer."
5. **Verification:** Hold your right hand out again, palm up, and ask the audience to verify that your hand is still empty. This is your second misdirection.
