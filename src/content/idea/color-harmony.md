**Trick Name: Color Harmony**

**Materials:**

1. A small, sealed envelope with a note inside that contains a random color (e.g., red, blue, green, yellow, or any other color you prefer).
2. A deck of colored cards (you can use a standard deck of cards and replace the numbers with colors).
3. A small piece of paper and a pen for the prediction.
4. A small box or container with a lid (e.g., a small wooden box or a plastic container).

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Choose a random color from the sealed envelope and write it down on the piece of paper. Fold the paper and keep it in your pocket or a safe place.
2. Shuffle the deck of colored cards and place it on the table.
3. Explain to the audience that you will choose a color from the deck and write it down on a new piece of paper. You will then seal the paper in the small box and ask the audience to choose a color from the deck.

**Performance:**

1. Take the deck of colored cards and ask the audience to choose a color. Let them pick a card from the deck and show it to you.
