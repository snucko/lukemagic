Here's a unique mentalism trick that reads minds and predicts the future, perfect for beginners:

**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Oracle"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, blank piece of paper (about 3x5 inches)
2. A pen or pencil
3. A small, sealed envelope (about 3x5 inches)
4. A deck of ordinary playing cards (used only for shuffling and presentation)

**Preparation:**

1. Before the performance, write a simple message on the blank piece of paper, such as "You will meet someone new today" or "You will receive a surprise gift soon." Fold the paper and place it in the sealed envelope.
2. Shuffle the deck of playing cards and place it on the table.

**Performance:**

1. Ask a volunteer to choose a card from the deck. They should sign their name across the face of the card to ensure it's clearly identifiable as their selection.
2. Ask the volunteer to place their signed card on top of the deck.
