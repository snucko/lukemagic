**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Connection"**

**Materials:**

* A standard deck of 52 playing cards
* A small piece of paper and a pen
* A willing participant (spectator)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Before the trick, secretly write a number (e.g., 5) on the piece of paper and fold it up so that the number is not visible. Place the paper in your pocket or a secure location.
2. **Shuffle the Deck:** Shuffle the deck of cards to mix them up.
