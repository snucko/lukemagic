**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Floating Garden"**

**Props:**

1. A beautiful, delicate glass terrarium (approx. 6-8 inches in diameter and 8-10 inches tall)
2. A small, artificial flower (with a magnetic base)
3. A small, ornate box (with a hidden compartment)
4. A small, ornate key
5. A small, velvet-covered cushion

**Setup:**

1. Place the glass terrarium on a table or pedestal in front of the audience.
2. Inside the terrarium, place a small amount of decorative rocks or pebbles.
3. Place the artificial flower on top of the rocks, with the magnetic base facing upwards.
4. Place the small, ornate box on the table or pedestal, next to the terrarium.
5. Place the small, ornate key on top of the box.
