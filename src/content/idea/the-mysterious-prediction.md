**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Prediction"**

**Materials:**

- A standard deck of 52 playing cards
- A small piece of paper or an index card
- A pen or pencil
- A sealed envelope (pre-addressed to the performer)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Before the performance, write a prediction on the piece of paper or index card. This prediction should be a specific card from the deck (e.g., the 8 of Hearts). Fold the paper and place it in the sealed envelope. Address the envelope to the performer and seal it.

2. **Performance:** Shuffle the deck of cards and ask a spectator to choose a card at random. They should sign their name across the face of the card to ensure it's their selection.

3. **The Prediction:** Ask the spectator to place their signed card on top of the deck. Then, ask them to cut the deck at a random point.

4. **The Reveal:** Take the sealed envelope from the spectator and open it. Inside, they'll find the prediction you wrote earlier. If the prediction matches the signed card, it's a stunning coincidence.
