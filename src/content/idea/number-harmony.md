**Trick Name: "Number Harmony"**

**Materials:**

1. A blank white card or piece of paper
2. A pen or pencil
3. A small piece of paper or envelope with a hidden compartment (you can use a simple envelope or create a hidden pocket in a card)
4. A set of 6 small cards or pieces of paper with numbers 1-6 written on them in a different color (you can use a marker or colored pens)
5. A small box or container with a lid (e.g., a small wooden box or a plastic container)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Write a prediction on the blank white card or paper, such as "The numbers 3, 5, and 2 will be chosen."
2. Place the prediction in the hidden compartment of the envelope or card.
3. Shuffle the 6 small cards or pieces of paper with numbers 1-6 written on them and place them in the small box or container.
