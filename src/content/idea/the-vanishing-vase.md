**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Vase"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, lightweight vase (preferably made of glass or plastic)
2. A handkerchief or a small piece of cloth
3. A small, hidden compartment or a secret pocket in your jacket or vest
4. A small amount of dry ice (optional, but recommended for a more dramatic effect)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. Begin by placing the vase on a table or a pedestal in front of the audience.
2. Cover the vase with the handkerchief, making sure to conceal it completely.
3. Hold the handkerchief in one hand, with the vase underneath.
4. Slowly lift the handkerchief, making it seem like the vase is rising into the air.
5. As the handkerchief is lifted, use your other hand to secretly place the vase into the hidden compartment or secret pocket in your jacket or vest.
6. Keeping the handkerchief in the air, pretend to search for the vase, looking around the table and under the handkerchief.
7. After a few moments of searching, suddenly drop the handkerchief, making it seem like the vase has vanished into thin air.
