**Trick Name: "The Time Capsule"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, sealed envelope or a small, decorative box with a lid (you can use a small treasure chest or a decorated box).
2. A pen or a pencil.
3. A piece of paper or a small notepad.
4. A small piece of string or a rubber band.
5. A deck of cards (optional).

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Before the performance, write a series of numbers (1-10) on the piece of paper or notepad. Fold the paper and place it inside the envelope or box. Seal it with the string or rubber band.
2. If you're using a deck of cards, shuffle them and place them on a table or in a designated area.
