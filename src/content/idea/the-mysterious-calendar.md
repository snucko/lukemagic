Here's a unique mentalism trick idea suitable for beginners:

**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Calendar"**

**Materials:**

1. A small calendar with a grid of days (you can use a printed calendar or create one on a piece of paper).
2. A pen or pencil.
3. A small piece of paper or a sticky note.
4. A sealed envelope or a small box.

**Effect:**

The performer claims to predict the future by reading the participant's mind. The participant chooses a date on the calendar, and the performer reveals a secret message that corresponds to that date.

**Steps:**

1. **Preparation:** Before the performance, write a series of cryptic messages on the small piece of paper or sticky note, each corresponding to a specific date on the calendar. For example:
	* January 1st: "New beginnings"
	* January 15th: "Midpoint"
	* February 14th: "Love is in the air"
	* And so on...
2. **Performance:** Show the participant the calendar and ask them to choose a date at random.
3. **The Secret:** While the participant is choosing the date, secretly glance at the calendar and remember the date they chose.
