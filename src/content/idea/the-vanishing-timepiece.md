**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Timepiece"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, thin, and flat watch or a pocket watch (preferably with a chain)
2. A small, folded piece of paper or a business card
3. A table or other flat surface
4. A pen or pencil

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**/*Before the Trick Begins:*/**

1. Place the watch on the table in front of you, with the face of the watch visible to the audience.
2. Take the folded piece of paper or business card and place it on top of the watch, covering it completely.
3. Hold the paper in place with one hand, and with the other hand, take the pen or pencil and write a simple message on the paper, such as "Time stands still" or "The clock is ticking."

**/*The Trick Begins:*/**

1. Show the audience the message you wrote on the paper, and then place the paper back on top of the watch.
2. Hold the paper in place with one hand, and with the other hand, slowly lift the watch off the table, keeping it hidden behind the paper.
