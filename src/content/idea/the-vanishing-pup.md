**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Pup"**

**Materials:**

1. A 10-15 foot long piece of colored string or twine (preferably with a bit of texture)
2. A small stuffed animal or toy (less than 2 inches in size)
3. A clear plastic or glass container with a flat bottom (e.g., a small glass vase or a plastic container)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Tie one end of the string to the top of the container, leaving about 6-8 inches of string hanging loose.
2. Place the small stuffed animal or toy near the other end of the string, but not tied to it.
3. Practice handling the string and container to get a feel for the weight and balance.

**Performance:**

1. Hold the container in one hand, with the string hanging loose.
2. Show the audience the stuffed animal or toy sitting on the table or stage, near the end of the string.
3. Explain that you're going to make the toy disappear, and ask the audience to watch closely.
4. Hold the container up, and with a flourish, swing the string over the toy, as if you're "lassoing
