**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Sugar Cube"**

**Materials:**

1. 3 small cups or containers (e.g., small ceramic cups, shot glasses, or even empty tin cans)
2. 1 sugar cube
3. A small piece of cloth or a handkerchief
4. A table or surface to perform on

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Place one of the cups in the center of the table, and the other two cups on either side of it.
2. Place the sugar cube on top of the center cup.
3. Fold the cloth or handkerchief into a small square and place it on top of the sugar cube.

**Performance:**

1. Show the audience the sugar cube and the three cups.
2. Place your hand on top of the center cup, and with a flourish, lift the cup off the table.
3. Keeping your hand on top of the cup, slowly lower it back down onto the table, but this time, place it on top of one of the side cups.
4. Keeping your hand on top of the cup, slowly lift the side cup off the table, and to the audience's surprise, the sugar cube has vanished!
