**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Balloon"**

**Materials:**

1. 3 small plastic cups or containers (e.g., yogurt cups)
2. 1 small balloon (any color)
3. 1 small piece of string or twine
4. 1 small weight or marble (optional)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Blow up the balloon to a small size and tie a knot at the end to prevent air from escaping.
2. Place the balloon under one of the cups, making sure it's centered and not visible from the top.
3. Take the string or twine and tie it around the neck of the cup, securing the balloon in place.
4. Position the two other cups on either side of the cup with the balloon, creating a row of three cups.
