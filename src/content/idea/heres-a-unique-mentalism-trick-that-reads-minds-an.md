Here's a unique mentalism trick that reads minds and predicts the future, perfect for beginners:

**Trick Name: "The Future's Path"**

**Materials:**

* A small, blank piece of paper (about 3x5 inches)
* A pen or pencil
* A small, sealed envelope (about 3x5 inches)
* A deck of playing cards (optional)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Before the performance, write a series of numbers (1-10) on the blank piece of paper, fold it, and place it inside the sealed envelope. Make sure the numbers are not visible through the envelope.
2. **Performance:** Ask a volunteer to choose a number between 1 and 10. You can use a deck of playing cards to help them make their choice (e.g., suit and number).
3. **The Prediction:** Ask the volunteer to think of a specific number and write it down on a piece of paper. Then, ask them to fold the paper and give it to you.
4. **The Reveal:** Open the sealed envelope and show the volunteer that the number on the paper inside matches the number they wrote down.
