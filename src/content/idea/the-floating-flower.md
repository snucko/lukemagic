**Trick Name: "The Floating Flower"**

**Props:**

1. A beautiful, delicate flower (preferably a silk or artificial one) attached to a thin, transparent thread or fishing line.
2. A small, decorative box with a hinged lid (about 6-8 inches in size).
3. A small, hidden compartment or a secret drawer in the box (can be created using a false bottom or a hidden panel).
4. A small, soft, and lightweight object (such as a small piece of fabric or a small stuffed animal) to serve as a "decoy" object.
5. A table or a pedestal to display the box.

**Setup:**

1. Place the small object (decoy) on the table or pedestal, making sure it's visible to the audience.
2. Open the box and show the audience that it's empty.
3. Close the box and attach the flower to the thread or fishing line, making sure it's hidden from view.
4. Place the box on the table or pedestal, next to the decoy object.

**Performance:**

1. Invite a volunteer from the audience to come on stage and hold the box.
2. Explain that the goal is to make the flower
