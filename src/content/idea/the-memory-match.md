**Trick Name: "The Memory Match"**

**Materials:**

- A standard deck of 52 playing cards
- A small piece of paper and a pen
- A table or surface for performance

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Shuffle andertoire**: Shuffle the deck of cards and have the spectator mix it as well. This ensures the cards are in a random order.
2. **The Challenge**: Explain to the spectator that you will try to remember the exact order of the top 5 cards of the deck. Ask them to choose 5 cards from the top of the deck and place them face down on the table.
3. **The Memory Test**: Take a small piece of paper and write down the exact order of the 5 chosen cards as you remember them. Make sure to write the cards in the order they were placed on the table, from left to right.
4. **The Reveal**: Ask the spectator to turn over the 5 cards they chose and place them in the same order on the table. Then, reveal your written list of the 5 cards. To add an element of surprise, you can ask the spectator to shuffle the remaining cards in the deck and then deal a new 5-card hand
