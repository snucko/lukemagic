**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Book"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, thin book (around 3-4 inches in height and 5-6 inches in width) with a blank cover.
2. A small piece of paper or a business card with a simple message (e.g., "The answer is inside").
3. A small, hidden compartment in the book (you can create one using a thin piece of cardboard or a small envelope).
4. A small, thin ribbon or a piece of string.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Before the performance, place the small piece of paper or business card with the message inside the hidden compartment in the book. Make sure the compartment is securely closed.
2. **Performance:** Hold the book in one hand and the ribbon or string in the other. Show the audience the book and explain that you will reveal a secret message inside.
3. **The Vanishing Book:** Slowly open the book, allowing the audience to see the blank cover. As you open the book, secretly drop the ribbon or string into the hidden compartment.
