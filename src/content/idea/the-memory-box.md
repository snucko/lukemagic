Here's a unique mentalism trick that can be learned by beginners:

**Trick Name: "The Memory Box"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, decorative box with a lid (you can use a wooden or plastic box)
2. A set of 5-6 small, blank cards or index cards
3. A pen or pencil
4. A small piece of paper or a sticky note
5. A random object (e.g., a small toy, a coin, or a button)

**Preparation:**

1. Before the performance, write a number (1-6) on each of the blank cards. Fold the cards and mix them up.
2. Place the cards in the box and close the lid.
3. Think of a random object (e.g., a small toy, a coin, or a button) and place it in the box with the cards.
4. Close the box and give it a few shakes to mix up the cards and the object.

1. Show the box to the audience and ask them to choose a number between 1 and 6.
