**Trick Name: "The Colorful Enigma"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, sealed envelope (can be a standard business envelope)
2. A piece of paper with a grid of 6 colors (red, blue, green, yellow, purple, and orange)
3. A pencil or pen
4. A small piece of paper with a random number between 1 and 6 (written on it)
5. A colored marker or pen

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Before the performance, write a random number between 1 and 6 on the small piece of paper. Fold it and keep it in your pocket or a secure location.
2. Create the grid of colors on the piece of paper with a pencil or pen. Ensure that the colors are evenly spaced and the grid is clear.
3. Seal the envelope with a small piece of tape or a sticker.

**Performance:**

1. Show the sealed envelope to the audience and ask them to choose a color from the grid.
