**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Coin in the Vase"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, empty vase or a clear glass container (approx. 5-6 inches tall)
2. A single coin (any denomination, but a small one works best)
3. A handkerchief or a small piece of cloth
4. A table or surface to perform on

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Place the vase on the table in front of you.
2. Secretly place the coin on the back of your hand, near your wrist, and close your fingers over it.

**Performance:**

**Phase 1: The Coin in the Vase**

1. Hold the vase in one hand and use your other hand to place the handkerchief over the top of the vase.
2. Slowly lift the handkerchief, making sure to keep the coin hidden in your hand.
3. As you lift the handkerchief, use your fingers to secretly drop the coin into the vase.
4. Show the audience that the coin is now in the vase by lifting the handkerchief and pointing to the vase.
