**Trick Name: "The Curious Connection"**

**Materials:**

- A standard deck of 52 playing cards
- A small piece of paper (about 3x5 inches)
- A pen or pencil
- A small wooden or plastic box (about 2x2x1 inches) with a lid

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Shuffle and Prepare the Deck:** Shuffle the deck of cards and place it on the table in front of you. Ask a spectator to choose a card from the deck and sign their name across it to identify it. This will be their "signature card."

2. **Write a Prediction:** On the small piece of paper, write a short message, such as "Your card is connected to the number 7." Fold the paper and place it in the small box.

3. **The Connection:** Ask the spectator to place their signature card on top of the deck. Then, ask them to cut the deck at a random point. Take the top card from the deck and place it on top of the deck again. This card will be the "connection card."
