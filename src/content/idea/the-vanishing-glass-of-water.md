**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Glass of Water"**

**Materials:**

1. 3 empty glasses or cups
2. 1 small glass or cup with a narrow neck (for the "magic" glass)
3. 1 small cup or container with a lid (for the "mystery" container)
4. 1 small amount of water
5. A tablecloth or other cloth to cover the table

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Place the 3 empty glasses in a row on the table, with the narrow-necked glass in the middle. Place the small cup or container with a lid at the end of the row.
2. **Pours the Water:** Pour a small amount of water into the narrow-necked glass.
3. **Cover the Table:** Quickly cover the table with the tablecloth or cloth to conceal the glasses.
4. **The Vanishing Act:** Lift the tablecloth and show the audience that the narrow-necked glass is empty. Ask the audience to confirm that the water has vanished.
5. **The Reveal:** Lift the small cup or container with a lid and show the audience that the water has reappeared inside the container.
