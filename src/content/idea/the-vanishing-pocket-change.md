**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Pocket Change"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, flat, and smooth object (such as a key, a small piece of metal, or a flat stone) *not a coin*
2. A small piece of cloth or a handkerchief
3. A pocket or a small container to hold the object
4. A willing participant (spectator)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Practice the sleight of hand by holding the small object in your dominant hand, and with your non-dominant hand, cover it with the cloth or handkerchief.
2. Make sure the object is completely hidden from view.

**Performance:**

1. Approach the spectator and ask them to hold out their hand.
2. Place the small object in their hand, making sure it's hidden under the cloth or handkerchief.
3. Hold the spectator's hand with your non-dominant hand, and with your dominant hand, place the cloth or handkerchief on top of their hand.
4. **Misdirection:** As you place the cloth on top of their hand, quickly glance at the object in their hand and say, "You have it now."
