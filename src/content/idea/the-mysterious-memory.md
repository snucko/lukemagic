**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Memory"**

**Materials:**

* A standard deck of 52 playing cards
* A small piece of paper and a pen
* A willing participant (spectator)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Prepare the Deck:** Shuffle the deck of cards and ask the spectator to choose a card at random. Have them sign their name across the face of the card to ensure it's clearly identifiable as their selection.
2. **The Memory Box:** Take the signed card and place it on top of the deck. Explain to the spectator that you'll create a "memory box" to store their card. To do this, place the deck on a flat surface and use your hands to create a small "box" shape around the top card (the signed selection).
3. **The Secret:** While maintaining the "box" shape, use your free hand to secretly palm the signed card. This can be done by using a gentle, sweeping motion to push the card into your hand.
4. **The Memory Test:** Ask the spectator to verify that their signed card is still on top of the deck. When they confirm, reveal the top card to show it's still their selection.
