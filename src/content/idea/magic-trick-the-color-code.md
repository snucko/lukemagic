**Magic Trick: "The Color Code"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, blank envelope (A6 or A7 size)
2. A sealed, numbered folder or a small box with six different numbers (1-6)
3. A set of six colored cards or slips of paper (red, blue, green, yellow, orange, and purple)
4. A piece of paper and a pen for the spectator to write on
5. A table or surface for the performance

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Before the performance, write a prediction on the piece of paper: "The color of the number [insert number] is [insert color]."
2. Fold the paper and place it in the sealed envelope.
3. Shuffle the colored cards or slips of paper and place them face down on the table.
4. Place the sealed folder or box with the numbers on the table.

**Performance:**

1. Explain to the spectator that you will perform a prediction trick using numbers and colors.
2. Ask the spectator to choose a number from the folder or box (1-6).
3. Once they have chosen a number, ask them to write it down on the piece of paper and keep it
