**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Spoon"**

**Materials:**

- 3 empty cups or small containers (e.g., coffee cups or small bowls)
- 1 spoon
- A table or flat surface
- A handkerchief or small piece of cloth

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Place the three cups in a row on the table, with the spoon on top of the middle cup. Make sure the spoon is centered and not touching the edges of the cup.

2. **The Setup:** Hold the handkerchief in one hand, with the spoon resting on top of it. Place the handkerchief over the spoon, covering it completely.

3. **The Misdirection:** Use your other hand to wave your fingers over the cups, as if you're about to perform a simple transfer. This will distract the audience from the spoon.

4. **The Transfer:** Quickly lift the handkerchief with the spoon underneath it, and place it on top of the right cup. Make sure the spoon is still covered by the handkerchief.
