**Trick Name: "Mystery Blossom"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, delicate flower (preferably a silk or artificial one that can be easily manipulated)
2. A small, hollow wooden or plastic box (about 2-3 inches in length and 1 inch in width)
3. A paper envelope or a small, decorative box with a hidden compartment
4. A small piece of tissue paper or a handkerchief
5. A small, hidden compartment or a secret pocket in your jacket or vest (optional)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Place the flower in the small box and close the lid.
2. Place the box in your pocket or a hidden compartment.
3. Place the paper envelope or decorative box with the hidden compartment in your other pocket or a visible location.
