**Trick Name: "Floating Treat"**

**Materials:**

1. 3-4 small cups or containers (such as glasses or small bowls)
2. A small, lightweight object (like a small toy, a marble, or a small piece of candy)
3. A tablecloth or a piece of cloth
4. A small, hidden compartment (you can use a small box or a hidden pocket in your tablecloth)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Place the small object (the "treat") in the hidden compartment.
2. Place the cups or containers on the table, in a row or a circle.
3. Cover the cups with the tablecloth or the piece of cloth.

**Performance:**

1. Ask a spectator to choose a cup to lift the tablecloth and reveal the treat.
2. As the spectator lifts the tablecloth, use misdirection to secretly move the treat to the chosen cup.
3. Once the spectator places the tablecloth back down, ask them to lift it againsembler to see the treat inside the chosen cup.
4. To add a surprising twist, you can have the treat "float" to a different cup by secretly moving it while the spectator is not looking
