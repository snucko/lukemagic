**'Floating Petals' Magic Trick**

This unique twist on a classic magic trick is perfect for beginners. It combines the mystique of flowers with a surprising reveal.

**Materials:**

- A small, artificial flower (with detachable petals)
- A small, clear plastic container (like a mini jar or vase)
- A small piece of clear tape
- A palm-sized, translucent sheet of plastic (like a plastic bag or a clear folder)
- A small, hidden compartment (like a button or a magnetic catch) in the container

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Place the artificial flower in the container, securing it with a small piece of clear tape. Attach the hidden compartment to the container, making sure it's not visible to the audience.

2. **Performance:** Hold the container in one hand and the translucent sheet of plastic in the other. Explain to the audience that you will make the flower petals float in mid-air.
