**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Number Sequence"**

**Materials:**

1. A deck of 10-12 numbered cards (you can use a regular deck of cards or create your own custom deck with numbers 1-12).
2. A small piece of paper and a pen.
3. A sealed envelope (optional).

**Objective:** To make it seem like you're reading the performer's mind and predicting a sequence of numbers.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Shuffle the deck of numbered cards and place them face down on a table.
2. Ask the performer to think of a sequence of 3-4 numbers between 1-12. You can give them a hint that the numbers should be in ascending order.
3. Have the performer write down their sequence on the piece of paper and fold it to conceal their answer.

**Performance:**

1. Ask the performer to hold the folded paper in their hand, but not to show you the numbers.
2. Take the deck of cards and ask the performer to choose one card at random from the deck. They should place the card on top of the deck.
3. Take the deck and ask the performer to choose another card at random from the deck
