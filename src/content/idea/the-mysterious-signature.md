**Trick Name: The Mysterious Signature**

**Materials:**

1. A standard deck of 52 playing cards
2. A pen or a marker
3. A piece of paper or a small notebook
4. A willing participant (spectator)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. Shuffle the deck of cards and ask the spectator to choose a card at random. They should sign their name across the face of the card to ensure it's clearly identifiable as their selection.
2. Take the signed card from the spectator and place it on top of the deck.
3. Ask the spectator to think of a number between 1 and 10. This number will serve as a "key" to the trick.
4. Take the deck and place it on a flat surface in front of you. Show the spectator that the signed card is still on top of the deck.
5. Ask the spectator to close their eyes and point to a card in the deck. They should point to a card without looking at it.
