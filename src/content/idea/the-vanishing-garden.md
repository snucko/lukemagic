**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Garden"**

**Props:**

1. A small, glass terrarium (approx. 6-8 inches in diameter)
2. A few small, artificial flowers (e.g., silk roses, daisies, or carnations)
3. A small, hidden compartment or a secret drawer (can be made from a wooden or plastic box)
4. A small bag or envelope (for the final reveal)
5. A table or stage with a cloth or tablecloth

**Setup:**

1. Place the glass terrarium on the table or stage, with the artificial flowers inside.
2. Secretly place the hidden compartment or secret drawer under the tablecloth, near the terrarium.
3. Place the small bag or envelope on the table, near the terrarium.

**Performance:**

**Phase 1: The Setup**

1. Show the audience the terrarium with the artificial flowers inside.
2. Explain that you will create a "garden" in the terrarium, using the flowers.
3. Place the terrarium on the table, and cover it with a cloth or tablecloth.

**Phase 2: The Vanishing**

1. Lift the cloth or tablecloth, and ask the audience
