**Trick Name: "The Dreamcatcher"**

**Materials:**

* A small, decorative bowl or container with a lid (e.g., a small vase or a decorative box)
* A small piece of paper and a pen
* A few small, colorful stones or beads
* A small, blank card or a piece of paper with a simple design (e.g., a small flower or a geometric shape)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Before the performance, write a simple message or a word on the small piece of paper, fold it, and place it in the bowl or container. Make sure the message is not visible to anyone.
2. **Performance:** Ask a volunteer to choose a small stone or bead from a bowl or a container. This will be their "dreamcatcher."
3. **The Prediction:** Ask the volunteer to hold the stone or bead in their hand and close their eyes. Explain that you will write a prediction on the blank card or paper, which will be related to their "dreamcatcher."
