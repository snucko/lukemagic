**Trick Name: "The Lost and Found Prediction"**

**Materials:**

- A standard deck of 52 playing cards
- A piece of paper and a pen
- A sealed envelope

**Preparation:**

1. Before performing the trick, write a prediction on the piece of paper. It should be a simple statement, such as "The selected card is the 8 of Hearts."
2. Fold the paper and seal it in the envelope. Make sure to keep the envelope sealed until the end of the trick.
3. Shuffle the deck of cards to mix them up.

**Performance:**

1. Ask a spectator to choose a card from the deck at random. You can use a simple method like asking them to pick a card from the top of the deck or draw one from the middle.
2. Once the spectator has chosen their card, ask them to sign their name across the face of the card to ensure it's clearly identifiable as their selection.
3. Take the signed card from the spectator and place it on top of the deck.
4. Shuffle the deck again to mix the cards. This time, ask the spectator to cut the deck at any point they like.
5. Once the spectator has cut the deck, take the top card
