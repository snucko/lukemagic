**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Coin in the Wooden Box"**

**Materials:**

1. A small wooden box (about 2 inches x 2 inches x 1 inch) with a hinged lid.
2. A small coin (such as a quarter or a half-dollar).
3. A handkerchief or a small piece of cloth.
4. A table or a surface to perform on.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Place the wooden box on the table in front of you.
2. Secretly place the coin inside the box, making sure it's not visible.
3. Close the lid of the box and tap it gently to make sure the coin is secure.

**Performance:**

**Phase 1: The Coin is Placed in the Box**

1. Hold the box in one hand and the coin in the other.
2. Show the coin to the audience and place it on top of the box.
3. Cover the coin with the handkerchief or cloth.
