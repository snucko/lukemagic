**Trick Name: "The Crystal Ball"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, clear glass or plastic orb (about 2-3 inches in diameter)
2. A folded piece of paper with a random word or phrase written on it (e.g., "sunflower," "Paris," or "happy")
3. A deck of 5-6 index cards with different numbers (1-6) written on each card
4. A small, hidden compartment or envelope
5. A pen or pencil

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Before the performance, secretly write a random word or phrase on the folded piece of paper. Fold the paper and place it in the hidden compartment or envelope.
2. Shuffle the deck of index cards and place them face down on a table or surface.
