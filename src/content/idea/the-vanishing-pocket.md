**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Pocket"**

**Materials:**

- A small, flat, and circular object (such as a coin, a button, or a small piece of metal)
- A pocket square or a small piece of cloth
- A table or a flat surface
- A willing participant (spectator)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Place the small object (coin) on the table in front of you. Make sure it's visible to the spectator.

2. **Initial Show:** Hold the pocket square in one hand, with the other hand palm-up on top of it. Place the coin on top of your palm, making sure it's hidden from view by the pocket square.

3. **Misdirection:** With your other hand, slowly lift the pocket square, creating a small, visible bulge. This will distract the spectator's attention away from the coin.

4. **Sleight of Hand:** As you lift the pocket square, use your fingers to secretly push the coin into your other hand, which is holding the pocket square. This movement should be quick and smooth.
