**Trick Name: "The Traveling Signature"**

**Materials:**

* A standard deck of 52 playing cards
* A pen or a marker
* A small piece of paper or a notepad
* A willing participant (spectator)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Before the trick, ask the spectator to sign their name on the small piece of paper or notepad. This will serve as a unique identifier for the trick.
2. **Shuffle the Deck:** Shuffle the deck of cards to mix them up. This will ensure that the cards are in a random order.
3. **Ask the Spectator to Choose a Card:** Ask the spectator to choose a card from the deck at random. Make sure they don't show you the card.
4. **Sign the Card:** Ask the spectator to sign their name on the back of the chosen card. This will create a connection between the card and the spectator's signature.
5. **Return the Card:** Ask the spectator to return the signed card to the deck.
6. **Mix the Deck Again:** Shuffle the deck again to mix up the cards.
7. **The Revelation:** Ask the spectator to look through the deck and find their signed card.
