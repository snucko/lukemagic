**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Clockwork Box"**

**Props:**

1. A small, wooden box with a hinged lid (approximately 6 inches long, 4 inches wide, and 2 inches deep).
2. A small, mechanical music box with a key (approximately 3 inches long, 2 inches wide, and 1 inch deep).
3. A small, decorative clock face (approximately 2 inches in diameter).
4. A small, transparent plastic container (approximately 2 inches long, 1 inch wide, and 1 inch deep) filled with small, colorful beads or marbles.
5. A small, hidden compartment in the box (approximately 1 inch long, 0.5 inches wide, and 0.5 inches deep).

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Place the mechanical music box inside the small, transparent plastic container.
2. Fill the hidden compartment in the box with a few small, colorful beads or marbles.
3. Attach the small, decorative clock face to the lid of the box using a small amount of glue or adhesive.
4. Practice opening and closing the box quickly and smoothly.
