**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Flower Petal"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, delicate flower (real or artificial) with petals that can be easily removed and rearranged.
2. A small, velvet pouch or a soft, drawstring bag.
3. A small, hidden compartment or a secret pocket in the pouch (can be created using a small piece of fabric or a hidden zipper).
4. A small, soft brush or a makeup brush.
5. A small, decorative stone or a small, shiny object.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Place the flower in the pouch, making sure the petals are not visible.
2. Create a small, hidden compartment or secret pocket in the pouch, where you can store the decorative stone or shiny object.
3. Practice hiding and revealing the stone or object in the hidden compartment.

**Performance:**

1. Hold the pouch in one hand, with the hidden compartment facing upwards.
2. Use the brush to gently sweep the petals of the flower out of the pouch, creating a small, delicate cloud of petals.
3. As you sweep the petals, use your other hand to secretly drop the decorative stone or shiny object into the hidden compartment.
