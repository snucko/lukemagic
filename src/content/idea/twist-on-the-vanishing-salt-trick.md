**Twist on the "Vanishing Salt" Trick**

**Magic Trick Name:** "663"

**Materials:**

1. A small, clear glass or plastic container with a lid (e.g., a mini jar or a small vase)
2. A small bag of salt (about 1-2 tablespoons)
3. A small piece of paper or a card with the number "663" written on it
4. A small, hidden compartment or a secret pocket (optional)

**Objective:** To make a small amount of salt seemingly disappear from a transparent container, leaving behind a surprising clue.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Fill the container with about 1-2 tablespoons of salt. Place the container on a table or a surface in front of the audience.
2. **Introduce the Number:** Show the audience the piece of paper or card with the number "663" written on it. Explain that this number holds a special significance.
