**Trick Name: "The Time Traveler's Deck"**

**Materials:**

- A standard deck of 52 playing cards
- A small piece of paper or a sticky note
- A pen or pencil
- A willing participant (spectator)

**Objective:** To create an illusion where a spectator can seemingly choose a card from the deck, and then have it appear in a different location on the deck after a series of magical manipulations.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Before the trick begins, secretly write the number of the spectator's chosen card on the piece of paper or sticky note. This will be used to reveal the card's location later.

3. **Card Placement:** Place the spectator's signed card on top of the deck, face up. This will be the starting point for the trick.
