**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Time Capsule"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, empty box or container with a lid (e.g., a wooden or plastic box, a small trunk, or a decorated tin)
2. A piece of paper and a pen
3. A small, sealed envelope (for the prediction)
4. A deck of ordinary playing cards (for a brief distraction)
5. A small, decorative clock or a watch (optional)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Before the performance, write a prediction on the piece of paper, such as a date, a number, or a simple phrase (e.g., "You will meet someone special today"). Fold the paper and place it inside the sealed envelope.
2. Place the sealed envelope in the box or container, making sure it's not visible to the audience.
3. Shuffle the deck of playing cards and place it on a nearby surface.

**Performance:**

1. Begin by explaining that you'll be performing a mentalism trick that will reveal a secret date or message from the future.
2. Ask a volunteer from the audience to choose a card from the deck
