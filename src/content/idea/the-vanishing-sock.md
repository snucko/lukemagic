**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Sock"**

**Materials:**

1. 2 small cups or containers (e.g., empty tin cans or small plastic cups)
2. 1 small ball or marble
3. 1 soft, small sock (preferably with a clear color)
4. A table or surface to perform on

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

**Preparation:**

1. Place one of the cups on the table in front of you.
2. Secretly place the small ball or marble in the cup.
3. Place the second cup on the table, slightly to the side of the first cup.
4. Place the small sock on the table, near the second cup.

**Performance:**

1. Hold the first cup in one hand and the second cup in the other hand.
2. Show the audience the sock, saying, "I'm going to place this sock in one of these cups."
3. Place the sock in the second cup, making sure to keep the ball or marble hidden in the first cup.
4. Hold the second cup in one hand and the first cup in the other hand, and ask the audience to choose which cup they think the sock is in.
5. Show the audience
