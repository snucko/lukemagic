**Trick Name: "The Vanishing Thread"**

**Materials:**

- A 10-15 foot long piece of colored string or twine (preferably with a subtle texture or pattern)
- A small metal or plastic ring (about 1-2 inches in diameter)
- A small piece of tape
- A handkerchief or small cloth (optional)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:** Before the performance, secretly attach the small ring to one end of the string. You can use a small piece of tape to hold it in place.

2. **The Setup:** Hold the string vertically in front of you, with the ring at the top. Loop the string around your hand, creating a small coil. This will be the "starting point" for the trick.

3. **The Performance:** Show the audience the looped string and the ring at the top. Explain that you will now "release" the ring into the air, and it will magically disappear.

4. **The Release:** Hold the string vertically and release the ring, allowing it to slide down the string. As the ring reaches the bottom of the string, quickly lift the string upwards, keeping the ring hidden.
