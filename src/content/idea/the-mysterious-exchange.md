**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Exchange"**

**Materials:**

* A standard deck of 52 playing cards
* A simple envelope or a small box with a lid (e.g., a matchbox)
* A pen or pencil

**Effect:**

The performer asks a spectator to choose a card from the deck and sign their name across it. The card is then placed in the envelope or box, and the spectator is asked to select another card from the deck. When the envelope or box is opened, the spectator finds that the signed card has been replaced by the second card they selected.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. Shuffle the deck of cards and ask a spectator to choose a card at random.
2. Ask the spectator to sign their name across the card, making sure to sign in a way that covers the entire card.
3. Take the signed card from the spectator and place it in the envelope or box.
4. Close the envelope or box and ask the spectator to select another card from the deck.
5. Take the second card from the spectator and place it on top of the deck.
6. Ask the spectator to open the envelope or box and find that the signed card has been replaced by the second card they selected
