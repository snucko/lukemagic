**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Match"**

**Materials:**

- A standard deck of 52 playing cards (without jokers)
- A calculator or a smartphone with a calculator app
- A piece of paper and a pen for each participant (optional)

**Effect:**

The performer asks a participant to choose a card from the deck, write its number on a piece of paper, and keep it secret. The performer then asks the participant to reveal the number of the chosen card. To everyone's surprise, the performer reveals the exact same number using a calculator or smartphone.

1. **Preparation:** Before the performance, shuffle the deck of cards and deal it to the participants. Make sure each participant has a full deck.
2. **The Selection:** Ask a participant to choose a card from their deck and write its number on a piece of paper. This number will be the target number.
3. **The Reveal:** Ask the participant to reveal the number they wrote on the paper. This is the target number.
