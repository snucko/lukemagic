**Trick Name: "The Mysterious Double Prediction"**

**Materials:**

1. A standard deck of 52 playing cards
2. A small piece of paper or a note card
3. A pen or pencil
4. A small envelope or a sealed envelope (optional)

**Objective:** To make the spectator choose a card, and then reveal two predictions that match their selection.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Preparation**: Before the performance, write two cryptic messages on the small piece of paper or note card. These messages should be related to the card values (e.g., a number or a suit). For example:
	* Message 1: "The number of hearts is 13"
	* Message 2: "The suit of the top card is red"
