**The Vanishing Key Trick: "Lost in the Aisle"**

**Materials:**

1. A small, old-fashioned key (preferably with a distinctive shape or design)
2. A small notebook or a sheet of paper
3. A pen or pencil
4. A small, hidden compartment (you can use a small wooden or plastic box, or even a hollowed-out book)
5. A set of three small, identical keys (optional)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. Begin by placing the small key in the hidden compartment. Make sure it's not visible to the audience.
2. Show the notebook or sheet of paper to the audience and write a simple message, such as "The key is hidden in the aisle."
3. Fold the paper or close the notebook, making sure the message is not visible.
4. Hold the folded paper or notebook in one hand, and with the other hand, pretend to search for the key in the hidden compartment.
